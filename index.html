Expanding Our Community

Medium Small is an organization that holds hybrid gallery and networking events that help artists and designers grow their networks!

Learn More

What is Medium Small?!

We're a new organization that unites creatives of various backgrounds through group exhibitions focused on informal sketchwork. We understand Austin's need for networking events, so we decided to start creating shows with a diverse range of artists to bring their respective networks into a single space.

Our Goals

No Barriers

We want artists to network and show work with no worries or guesswork.

Networking

We want to help the community become more connected!

Scale
